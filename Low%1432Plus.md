## Vengeful Spirit
 - Get the geo rock after the well (20-22)[22]
 - Do aspid arena and get enough geo (32+)[18]
 - Get geo rock at the beginning of the next room (50)[18]
 - Buy crossroads stag (0)[-50]
 - Skip false knight and collect vengeful spirit (7-10)[10]

## Fungal Wastes Entrance
 - Go through ancestral mound (60)[50]
 - Get 1 or 2 casts to perform the slopeball to fungal
 - Make sure you are capped at 50fps to make the trick consistent
 - Turn off fps cap and enter fungal wastes

## Mantis Claw Transition
 - If you have a fb left from the slopeball, save it for epogo
 - Collect as much free soul as you can (1, 4 and 2 respectively in the first 3 rooms)
 - Perform epogo by using 2 fbs
 - Collect some soul to perform mantis pogo and get claw (70)[12]
 - Dont sq, exit through the transition you came from

## Main Menu w/ Tram Pass
 - Grab the journal on the right {200}
 - Head left into deepnest and collect free soul on the way
 - Bench in hot springs
 - Go left, then up through the dark rooms (75)[7]
 - Kill the 2nd hatcher (100)[25]
 - Lure hatchers near tram pass, kill them after picking it up and sq (250)

## Dream Nail
 - Go left and kill some husks if you need the geo [7-10]
 - Navigate the dark rooms and kill 1 or 2 spiders if the husks didnt cut it [17-34]
 - Buy distant village stag and stag to crossroads (0-10)[-250]
 - Take the tram to resting grounds, collect dnail and sq

## Chandelier
 - Take the tram to basin
 - Head to city through broken bridge while collecting soul
 - Go straight up and kill the enemies above ghs (25)[19-28]
 - Kill the ghs and the lance sentry (85)[50+12]
 - Go up to wks while colleting soul and open the lever on the way for later
 - Break the chandelier

## Lurien
 - Dnail the wks beofre starting the fight if you need it
 - Kill wks and save any left over soul
 - Skip the chest and seal and absorb lurien

## Lemm
 - Get seal and the chest on the way down (740)[655]{450}
 - Go right after the elevator and go down towards the fountain collecting soul on the way
 - Kill the husks near the lever for geo (755)[18]
 - Talk to lemm and call the elevator for later
 - Perform rasfters skip with the vengefly and collect the seal {450}
 - Go right while collecting soul and open the lever next to lemm

## Lumafly Lantern
 - Sell all your relics to lemm and go right towards resting grounds (1855)[1100]
 - Need ~1890 geo, you best options are: (1890)
 	路 Kill 3 husks below spire [28] (~1.2s, -6 soul, ~27 geo)
	路 Kill two husks near the bottom of journal room [19] (~1s, save 1hp, -3 soul, ~18 geo)
 	路 Kill both husks right before the big elevator [18] (~0.9-1s, they can be annoying, -1 soul, ~17 geo)
	路 Kill the exploding husk in infected crossroads [15] (~0.5-1s, hard to greed with bad rng, -3 soul, ~15 geo)
 - Go up the elevator and through blue lake
 - Save sly and go up towards crossroads stag
 - Go left and kill 2 slobbering husks and 1 shield husk (1927)[40] (~1.3s, need 5-6 soul, ~37 geo)
 - Bench in fc station, stag to dirtmouth, buy lantern and sq (127)[-1800]

## Crystal Heart
 - Call the stag and go towards crystal peak while collecting soul
 - Buy the toll and go towards crystal heart (77)[-50]
 - Kill the glimback on the way if you really need the soul/geo/hp [6] (need at least 77 geo)
 - Make sure you enter gaunlet room with at least 6 soul to fb skip
 - Go through the gaunlet, collect cdash and sq

## Herrah
 - Stag to distant village and enter beast's den
 - Sit on the trap bench and make your way up towards herrah
 - Skip the first devout by dmg tanking, slashing its mask and pogoing him
 - Collect some soul on the spiders shortly after and then cdash
 - Keep collecting soul on the way up and make sure to have at least 2 hp and 9 soul for the last devout
 - Kill the last devout and absorb herrah (120)[43]

## Fog Canyon Entrance
 - Go left towards the exit and then up to stag to dirtmouth
 - Go down towards fog canyon while collecting soul
 - Perform an acid skip with cdash and enter fog canyon

## Monomon
 - Enter archives and fight uumuu (with 3 soul preferably)
 - Dnail him twice at the start and heal if you need it, making sure to start each dmg rotation with 6 soul
 - Kill uumuu in 3 cycles and absorb monomon

## THK Scream
 - Head out of archives while collecting soul
 - Go left towards queens station and buy the stag (0)[-120]
 - If you dont need the soul/hp, you can firebell before stagging to dmouth
 - Go down the well and towards black egg temple
 - Break the chains and start the thk fight

## The Hollow Knight
 - Fight him as best as you can
 - If you really need the hp, you can heal during the self stabs as you'll only deal 1 dmg per blow
 - Skip the last scream and finish him off
 - Absorb thk and wait for the final credits to roll
 - Make sure to show completion percent before you stop the recording
